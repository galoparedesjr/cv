Galo Paredes Jr

Founder of Casino Con El Pelon

Meet Galo, a passionate and experienced salsa dance instructor. He has been dancing salsa for over 10 years.

Galo specializes in Casino style salsa, and is also well-versed in other Latin dance styles such as Bachata and LA Style Salsa.

He is dedicated to helping his students improve their technique and style, while also fostering a fun and inclusive learning environment.

In addition to group classes, Galo also offers private lessons for individuals or couples looking to take their dancing to the next level.
